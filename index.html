How it works

Four steps to
staying on track

Tether wraps Apple's Screen Time framework in a layer of community — so you're never going cold-turkey alone.

1

Choose your apps

Select which apps or categories to restrict. Your choices stay on your device — never sent to our servers.

2

Invite your group

Add accountability partners — friends, a therapist, a coach, a recovery group. They're your approvers.

3

Request access

When you want temporary access to a blocked app, submit a request with a reason. Your group gets notified instantly.

4

Auto re-locks

If approved, the app unlocks for a fixed window — then automatically re-blocks. No willpower required to stop.
