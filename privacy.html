1. Information We Collect

Tether collects the minimum information necessary to provide accountability features. We do not sell your data to third parties.

  • Account information: email address and display name, used to identify you in your accountability group.
  • Group data: membership, roles, unlock requests, and approval decisions — shared only within your group.
  • Aggregate screen time totals by app category — never specific app logs or browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data is used exclusively to power Tether's accountability features: syncing group state, routing unlock requests, and generating usage summaries for your group.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide the service (e.g., AWS for hosting). Usage data is shared only within your chosen accountability group.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Authentication is handled via AWS Cognito. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the app or by contacting support. Deletion is completed within 30 days.
