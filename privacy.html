Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective May 12, 2026
Short version: Tether collects your email to notify you when the app launches, and — once you're a user — the minimum data needed to run your accountability group. We never sell your data, never see your browsing history, and app-blocking happens entirely on your device.

1. Who We Are

Tether ("we," "us," or "our") is an iOS application that uses Apple's Screen Time framework to help individuals and groups build healthier digital habits through mutual accountability. Our contact email is ryanlsmith191@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Before you download the app (waitlist):

  • Email address — collected via our waitlist form, processed by Formspree, Inc. Used only to notify you when Tether launches. We do not send marketing email without your consent.

When you use the app:

  • Account information — your email address and chosen display name, used to identify you within your accountability group. Authentication is handled by AWS Cognito; passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Group membership data — the members of your accountability group and their roles (member or approver).
  • Restriction preferences — which app categories you have chosen to restrict and your schedule settings. This data is stored on-device and synced to our servers only to restore your settings across devices.
  • Unlock request log — a record of each request you submit (app category, reason you provide, timestamp) and the approval or denial decision made by your group. This is the shared audit log that makes accountability possible.
  • Aggregate screen time totals — daily totals by app category (e.g., "45 minutes of Social Media"), derived from Apple's DeviceActivity framework. We never receive a list of specific apps you opened, individual session timestamps, or any browsing content.

What we never collect:

  • Specific apps you opened or browsed
  • URLs, search queries, or browser history
  • Location data of any kind
  • Contacts, call logs, or message content
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Your Apple ID or Screen Time passcode

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the accountability features of the app — routing unlock requests to your group, displaying usage summaries, maintaining the approval log.
  • To send you a launch notification email (waitlist only).
  • To restore your settings if you reinstall the app or switch devices.
  • To troubleshoot technical issues when you contact us for support.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

4. Apple Framework Data Handling

Tether uses three Apple frameworks that interact with Screen Time data:

  • FamilyControls — authorizes Tether to manage screen time restrictions. Authorization occurs through Apple's own system prompts. We do not receive your Apple ID or any credentials.
  • ManagedSettings — enforces app restrictions directly on your device. Restriction rules are applied locally and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • DeviceActivity — provides aggregate usage summaries. We receive category-level totals only (e.g., "Entertainment: 1h 20m"), not a log of individual app sessions.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Within your group — aggregate usage summaries and unlock request decisions are shared with the members of your chosen accountability group. You control who is in your group.
  • Service providers — AWS (hosting and authentication) and Formspree (waitlist form processing). Both are contractually bound to use your data only to provide services to us.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users.

6. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Data stored in our cloud infrastructure (AWS) is encrypted at rest using AES-256. We use AWS Cognito for authentication, which handles credential hashing and secure session management. No passwords are stored in plain text at any layer of our system.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the Tether app or by emailing ryanlsmith191@gmail.com. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently purge your personal information, group associations, and usage history from our infrastructure within 30 days.

Records you contributed to a shared group audit log are anonymized rather than deleted, so that the integrity of the shared history is preserved for other group members.

Waitlist email addresses are deleted upon your request at any time, or within 90 days of Tether's public launch.

8. Children's Privacy

Tether is designed for adults (18+) and is not a parental control tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion — request that your data be erased (see Section 7).
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from waitlist emails at any time using the link in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, email ryanlsmith191@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above and, where feasible, notify registered users by email. Continued use of Tether after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how we handle your data? Email us at ryanlsmith191@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
